AT YOUR SERVICE

CARING FOR YOUR WATCH

Life can be mostly hectic with the occasional lull; your daily routine shows on your timepiece. So allow us to guide you through some ways to keep your Vacheron Constantin piece in shape.

SERVICING

Our goal is to keep your watch as accurate and reliable as the day you got it. It's just a task our master watchmakers feel duty-bound to perform to the most exacting standards.

FIRST CLASS SERVICE CENTERS

Since 1755, our master watchmakers at Vacheron Constantin have been faithfully maintaining, repairing and restoring the timepieces you entrust to their care.

AUTHENTICATION

Arriving at the workshop, every piece follows a very precise process. First, they are meticulously identified to certify the authenticity. If necessary, the watchmakers call for the decisive opinion of the Heritage team, which proceeds to make checks using the brand's many archives.
